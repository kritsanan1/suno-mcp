# Suno AI Platform Overview

## What is Suno?

Suno is a revolutionary AI-powered music generation platform that enables users to create original songs from simple text prompts. Launched in 2023, Suno has quickly become one of the leading platforms in AI music generation, offering both amateur and professional musicians the ability to generate high-quality music tracks with minimal technical knowledge.
